Behind Bear René.

Established in 2006 and led by Founder Nicola Oaten, Bear René has developed a reputation for unique interiors and a personal service.

As champions of craftsmanship, we are supported by a select group of suppliers and artisans who share our love of classic design, allowing us to create individual and refined spaces.

Although aesthetics are at the forefront of our practice, how a space is experienced also depends on how well it functions. Our knowledge of both design and architecture allows us to create environments which are exciting yet practical.

Over the past 17 years, we are thrilled to have travelled with our clients to complete projects across the UK, as well as the US, Europe, Africa and Asia.

We are also proud to have had our designs featured in publications such as The World of Interiors, The Sunday Times, 25 Beautiful Homes and International Property & Travel.
